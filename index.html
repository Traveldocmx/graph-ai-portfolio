Why this course

I’m building a responsible GenAI toolkit to accelerate routine work while preserving rigor: transparent data workflows, auditable outputs, and high-quality scientific communication.

  • Design reusable prompts and workflows for analysis, synthesis, and reporting.
  • Create research-ready deliverables (docs, figures, presentations) with clear provenance.
  • Prototype automation for repetitive tasks while respecting privacy and safety constraints.
About the Course
An 8-week live cohort program focused on no-code AI tools for responsible task automation, analysis, and communication.

What it trains

The course emphasizes practical, ethical use of LLMs and automation tools to create structured outputs, accelerate research workflows, and build shareable assets.

  • Weekly live workshops, plus weekly help sessions and community support.
  • Personalized feedback on assessments and capstone project coaching.
  • Shareable certificate and lifetime access to tutorial materials.

Assessment

The grading rubric combines quizzes, workshop assignments, participation, and a final project. (Target: consistent weekly submissions and a high-quality, responsible capstone.)

Quizzes: 15% Assignments: 35% Participation: 10% Final project: 40%
